JOY OF GIVING WEEK 2025

Get ready to dive into a week of pure joy! ðŸŒŸ

Welcome to the Joy of Giving Week, where every act of kindness ripples into a sea of happiness! Join us as volunteers for the spectacular "Dish of Joy" and "Gift of Joy" events, and let's spread smiles that last a lifetime!

Imagine the joy on a child's face when they receive a surprise gift! Let's make that happen. Join us as we set up wish boxes in campus stationary shops. Pick a wish, grab a gift from the store, and fill those boxes with the magic of education for the kids in Zari and Lamani.

Last year, we brought smiles, and this year, with your help, we plan to make it even more incredible!

Foodies, unite! Indulge your love for food while making a difference. Partnering with campus eateries, we're turning delicious dishes into contributions for our cause. A portion of profits from your dish will go towards giving back to society. You'll be the hero spreading the word about these special dishes and ensuring the success of our mission.

Let's feast for a good cause!

What will you as a volunteers do?
  • Set up boxes and make wish chits which will be set up in all the stationery shops in campus.
  • Give away these gifts to lovely kids of Zari & Lamani.
  • Convince the food stalls to donate a portion of the proceeds from certain food items to Nirmaan.
  • Decide which food items will come under Dish of Joy.
  • Create an accountability system for the number of items sold by the stalls.
  • Promote the event through various means to have the greatest impact.
